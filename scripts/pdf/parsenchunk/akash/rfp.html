Fairfax County Government (“FCG”): Fairfax County, Virginia is located in the northeast
corner of Virginia and has an area of approximately 395 square miles with a diverse population

communications systems that are implemented in County government agencies throughout the

deploying information technology, and provides technology infrastructure, and

scope, and approving project expenditures. DIT assigns a technical PM who is

support on the technical implementation processes.
