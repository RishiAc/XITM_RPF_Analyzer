1. Offeror’s Financial Proposal must contain all price information in the format specified in the
Bid/Price Proposal Form and/or the requested total price. Do not amend, alter, or leave blank (as
applicable) any items on the Bid/Price Proposal Form, or include additional clarifying or contingent
language. Failure to adhere to any of these instructions may result in the proposal being determined
to be not reasonably susceptible of being selected for award.
a. Offeror shall propose fully loaded hourly rates that include all direct and indirect costs and
profit for the Offeror resource to perform. Indirect costs shall include all costs that would
normally be considered general and administrative costs and/or travel costs, or which in any
way are allocated by the Offeror against direct labor hours as a means of calculating profit
or recouping costs which cannot be directly attributable to the Contract.
