49. METHOD AND CONTAINERS-Unless otherwise specified, goods shall be delivered in commercial packages in standard
commercial containers that are constructed to ensure acceptance by common or other carrier for safe transportation to the point
of delivery. Containers become the property of the County unless otherwise specified by bidder.
50. REPLACEMENT-Materials or components that have been rejected by the Purchasing Agent, in accordance with the terms of a
contract, shall be replaced by the Contractor at no cost to the County.
