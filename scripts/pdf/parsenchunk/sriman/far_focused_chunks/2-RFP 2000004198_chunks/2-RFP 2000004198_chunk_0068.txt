18.9. Should the County determine in writing and in its sole discretion that only one Offeror is fully
qualified, or that one Offeror is clearly more highly qualified than the others under consideration,
a contract may be negotiated and awarded to that offeror. Each SAC will make appropriate
recommendations to the County Purchasing Agent, if appropriate, prior to the actual award of
contract.
18.10. Proposals which, after discussion and submission of additional clarification and/or
supplementary information, are determined to meet the specifications of this RFP will be
classified as "acceptable". Proposals found not to be acceptable will be classified as
"unacceptable" and no further discussion concerning same will be conducted.
