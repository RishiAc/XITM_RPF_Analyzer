23. DELAYS AND SUSPENSIONS:
23.1. The County may direct the Contractor, in writing, to suspend, delay, or interrupt all or any part
of the work of this contract for the period of time deemed appropriate for the convenience of
the County. The County will extend the Contractor's time of completion by a period of time that
in the discretion of the County Purchasing Agent is reasonably suited for completion of work.
