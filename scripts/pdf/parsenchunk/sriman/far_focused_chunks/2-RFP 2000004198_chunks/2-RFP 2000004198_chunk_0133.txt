47. POINT OF DESTINATION-All materials shipped to the County must be shipped F.O.B. DESTINATION unless otherwise stated
in the contract or purchase order. The materials must be delivered to the "Ship to" address indicated on the purchase order.
48. ADDITIONAL CHARGES-Unless bought F.O.B. "shipping point" and Contractor prepays transportation, no delivery charges
shall be added to invoices except when express delivery is authorized and substituted on orders for the method specified in the
contract. In such cases, difference between freight or mail and express charges may be added to invoice.
