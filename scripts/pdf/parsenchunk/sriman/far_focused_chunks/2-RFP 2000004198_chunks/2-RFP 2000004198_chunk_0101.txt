including the bid Cover Sheet and Pricing Schedule(s); (ii) properly signed in ink in the identified spaces; and (iii) submitted in a
sealed envelope or package.
If the bid prices or any other submissions differ on the copy of the submitted bid, the ORIGINAL copy shall prevail.
