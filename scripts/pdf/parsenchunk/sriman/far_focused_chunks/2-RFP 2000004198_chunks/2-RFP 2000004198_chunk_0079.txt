19.15. Nothing contained in the specifications shall be construed as creating any contractual
relationship between any subcontractor and the County. The Contractor is as fully responsible
to the County for the acts and omissions of the subcontractors and of persons employed by the
Contractor as it is for acts and omissions of person directly employed by Contractor.
19.16. Precaution shall be exercised at all times for the protection of persons (including employees)
and property.
19.17. The Contractor and all subcontractors are to comply with applicable federal, state, and local
occupational safety and health requirements, including, but not limited to, the Occupational
Safety and Health Act of 1970, Public Law 91-596, as it may apply to this contract.
