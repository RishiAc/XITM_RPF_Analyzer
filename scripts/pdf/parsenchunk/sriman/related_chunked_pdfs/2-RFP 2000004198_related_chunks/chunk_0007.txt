d. Professional Liability Insurance in the amount of $1,000,000 per claim to cover each
individual professional staff.
19.3. Liability Insurance “Claims Made” basis:

--- limits of insurance or additional insurance coverage against other hazards for which insurance ---
limits of insurance or additional insurance coverage against other hazards for which insurance
is reasonably obtainable and which, at the time, are commonly insured against in the case of
similar properties conducting similar activities within the geographic area of the Property,

--- e. Pay for all permits, licenses and fees and give all notices and comply with all laws, ordinances, rules, regulations, and ---
e. Pay for all permits, licenses and fees and give all notices and comply with all laws, ordinances, rules, regulations, and
policies of the County.

--- c. All work and services rendered in strict conformance to all laws, statues, and ordinances and the applicable government ---
c. All work and services rendered in strict conformance to all laws, statues, and ordinances and the applicable government
rules, regulations, methods, and procedures.

--- 1. The Notice of Suspension shall state the reasons for the actions taken and such decision shall be final unless the person or ---
1. The Notice of Suspension shall state the reasons for the actions taken and such decision shall be final unless the person or
firm appeals within ten (10) days of receipt of the Notice by instituting legal action as provided in the Code of Virginia.

--- 2. The Notice of Debarment shall state the reasons for the actions taken and the decision shall be final unless the person or ---
2. The Notice of Debarment shall state the reasons for the actions taken and the decision shall be final unless the person or
firm appeals within ten (10) days of receipt of the Notice by instituting legal action as provided in the Code of Virginia.

--- monetary relief or damages from the County must submit its claim to the Board of Supervisors in compliance with Virginia Code ---
monetary relief or damages from the County must submit its claim to the Board of Supervisors in compliance with Virginia Code
§ 15.2-1243 through 1249.
