1. Successful Offeror may be required to submit the following information for AOC engagements for
Successful Offeror personnel, subcontractors, third-party resources, temporary employees, and
training candidates:
a. Full Name.
b. Phone Number.
c. Personal/Private email with no connection to employer.
d. Emergency contact information.
