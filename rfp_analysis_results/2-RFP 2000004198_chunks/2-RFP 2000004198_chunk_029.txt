16. ADDENDA:
16.1. Offerors are reminded that changes to the proposal, in the form of addenda, are often issued
between the issue date and within three (3) days before the due date. All addenda shall be
signed and submitted as instructed on the addenda.
16.2. The last day to submit questions to be addressed in the addendum will be addressed in Bonfire
(Euna) under “Questions Due Date.” All questions pertaining to this RFP shall be submitted to
dpmmteam1@fairfaxcounty.gov. Questions submitted in Bonfire (Euna) will not be
considered, therefore, please make sure to send your questions to the e-mail address
above.
16.3. Notice of addenda will be posted on eVA and Bonfire (Euna). It is the Offeror’s responsibility to
monitor the web page for the most current addenda at https://fairfaxcounty.bonfirehub.com.
