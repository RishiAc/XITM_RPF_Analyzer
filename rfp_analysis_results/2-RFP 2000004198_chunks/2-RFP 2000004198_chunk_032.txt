19. INSURANCE:
19.1. The Contractor is responsible for its work and every part thereof, and for all materials, tools,
equipment, appliances, and property of any and all description used in connection therewith.
The Contractor assumes all risk of direct and indirect damage or injury to the property or
persons used or employed on or in connection with the work contracted for, and of all damage
or injury to any person or property wherever located, resulting from any action, omission,
commission or operation under the contract.
19.2. The Contractor shall, during the continuance of all work under the contract provide the
following:
a. Statutory Workers' Compensation and Employer's Liability insurance in limits of not less
than $1,000,000 to protect the Contractor from any liability or damages for any injuries
(including death and disability) to any and all of its employees, including any and all liability
or damage which may arise by virtue of any statute or law in force within the
Commonwealth of Virginia.
b. Commercial General Liability insurance in the amount of $1,000,000 per occurrence/
$2,000,000 in aggregate, to protect the Contractor, its sub-Contractors, and the interest of
the County, its officers, employees and agents against any and all injuries to third parties,
including bodily injury and personal injury, wherever located, resulting from any action or
operation under the Contract or in connection with the contracted work.
